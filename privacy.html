Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 December 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Budget Boss ("the bot") collects, uses, and stores information when you use it through Telegram and connect your Google account.

By using the bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in line with this policy.

2. Information we collect

2.1 From Telegram

  • Your Telegram user ID and username.
  • The messages you send to the bot that are needed to record your expenses.

2.2 From Google

When you sign in with Google, the bot receives an authorization code and exchanges it for access tokens using Google's OAuth 2.0 system. Based on the scopes you approve, the bot may access:

  • Basic profile information For example, your Google account name and email address, used to link your Telegram account to your Google account.
  • Google Sheets data The bot can create and update spreadsheets that store your expense entries. It only reads and writes the sheets that it manages for you.

The bot does not access any other Google data beyond the scopes you grant.

3. How we use the information

  • To record and update your expense entries in your Google account.
  • To identify you as a user and link your Telegram account to your Google account.
  • To reply to your commands and messages inside Telegram.

4. How we store and protect data

  • OAuth tokens and user identifiers may be stored on a secure server or database so that the bot can access your Google data when you ask it to.
  • Tokens are stored in a protected environment that is not shared with other applications.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing.

The expense data itself is stored in your own Google account, for example in Google Sheets. You can manage and delete this data directly in your Google account at any time.

5. How long we keep your data

  • OAuth tokens are kept for as long as you use the bot, so that it can access your Google data on your behalf.
  • If you stop using the bot, you can request deletion of your stored tokens and related records.
  • You can also revoke access from your Google Account, which stops all future access from the bot.

6. How you can revoke access and request deletion

6.1 Revoke Google access

  • Go to your Google Account settings, open the "Security" section, then "Third party access".
  • Find the app associated with Budget Boss and remove its access.
  • After this, the bot can no longer read or write any data in your Google account.

6.2 Request deletion of stored data

You can request deletion of any stored OAuth tokens and logs related to your use of the bot by contacting:

  • Email: you@example.com

In your request, please include your Telegram username and the Google email address you used with the bot so that we can locate your data.

7. Children

This bot is intended for use by adults. It is not designed to be used by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about how Budget Boss uses Google data, you can contact:

  • Email: you@example.com
