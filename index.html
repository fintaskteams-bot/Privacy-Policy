Budget Boss

Budget Boss is a Telegram bot that helps you track and organize your personal expenses using your Google account.

What this Telegram bot does

Budget Boss is a personal finance helper that runs inside Telegram. You can send simple text messages like "Coffee 120" or "Groceries 800" to the bot. The bot records these entries in your Google account so that you can view and analyze them later.

The goal is to make expense tracking easy, quick, and always available from your phone.

How it works

  • You start a chat with @Budget_Boss_bot> on Telegram.
  • The bot sends you a link to sign in with your Google account.
  • Google shows you a consent screen that explains what data the bot will access and asks for your permission.
  • After you grant access, the bot receives a secure token and uses it to write your expense entries into your Google account, for example a Google Sheet or another Google service you choose.
  • You can then continue to log expenses through Telegram. The bot uses your tokens to update your data until you revoke access.

What Google data the bot accesses

The bot uses Google APIs only after you grant explicit consent on the Google sign in page. Depending on how you configure the bot, it may request some or all of the following:

  • Basic profile information This includes your Google account name and email address. It is used to identify your account and link your Telegram user to the correct Google user.
  • Google Sheets access (for example https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets) This scope is used to create and update a spreadsheet that stores your expense records. The bot only reads and writes the specific sheets that it manages for you.

The bot does not access any other Google data outside of the scopes that you approve. You can see and manage these permissions in your Google Account settings at any time.

For full details about how data is handled, please read the Privacy Policy.

Your control and data revocation

  • You can revoke the bot's access to your Google account at any time from your Google Account settings under "Security" and "Third party access".
  • Once access is revoked, the bot can no longer read or write any of your Google data.
  • You can also request deletion of stored tokens and logs as described in the Privacy Policy.
